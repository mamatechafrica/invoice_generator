FAQ & Privacy

Is my data private?

Yes! Everything you type stays on your device. This app uses your browser's localStorage — your data is never sent to any server, database, or third party. Even we can't see it.

Can other people see my invoices?

No. Each browser stores its own data. Someone on a different computer or browser visiting the same URL will see a blank slate — not your information.

What happens if I clear my browser data?

Your saved settings and clients will be lost. To avoid this, download your invoices as PDF or HTML, and keep a copy of your settings (write them down or screenshot).

Can I use this offline?

Yes! Download the files from GitHub and open index.html in your browser. It works without any internet connection.

How do I send an invoice to my client?

Click Print/PDF and save as PDF, then email it as an attachment. Or use the Email button to open your mail app with a pre-filled message — attach the downloaded HTML invoice.

Is this free?

Yes, completely free. No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. The source code is open on GitHub for anyone to use, inspect, or modify.
