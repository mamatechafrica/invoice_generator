FAQ & Privacy

What happens if I clear my browser data?

Your saved info will be wiped. To keep a backup: click Print/PDF and save your invoice, or jot down your details somewhere safe.

How do I send an invoice to my client?

Click Print/PDF and save as PDF, then email it as an attachment. Or use the Email button to open your mail app with a pre-filled message — attach the downloaded HTML invoice.

Is this free?

Yes, completely free. No sign-ups, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. The source code is open on GitHub for anyone to use, inspect, or modify.
